Frequently Asked Questions

What are electricity tariff bands in Nigeria?

Tariff bands (A–E) classify customers based on average daily power supply hours.

Is this electricity bill calculation exact?

No. This is an estimate based on published average tariff rates. Actual bills may vary by DISCO and meter type.

Do you collect my city or state information?

No. All inputs stay in your browser. We do not store or transmit any data.

How can I reduce my electricity bill?

Use energy-efficient appliances, switch off unused devices, and monitor your monthly kWh consumption.
