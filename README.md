Naija Power Bill Calculator

A simple, fast, and free Nigerian electricity bill estimator based on official tariff bands (A–E).
This tool helps Nigerians estimate their monthly electricity cost using kWh consumption and current average NERC tariff rates. It runs entirely in the browser and does not collect or store any personal data.

What this app does

Calculates estimated electricity bills in Naira (₦)
Supports Nigerian tariff bands A–E
Allows custom ₦/kWh rate override
Works for prepaid and postpaid estimates
Light and Dark mode support
Mobile-friendly and fast

Who it is for

Nigerian households
Students and renters
Small business owners
Anyone tracking electricity expenses

How it works

Select your tariff band
Enter total kWh consumed for the month
Optionally enter your city and state
Instantly see your estimated bill

Accuracy note

Results are estimates based on average published tariff rates. Actual bills may vary by DISCO, meter type, and regulatory updates.

Privacy

No data is collected, stored, or transmitted.
